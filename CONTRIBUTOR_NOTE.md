## English

Hello everyone,

I am Runyuan Wang. I am a dietitian and also an astronomy enthusiast. In the past, I mostly used AI for chatting, image generation, and looking up information. Now I am trying to explore whether AI can help with nutrition education and astronomy outreach in practical ways.

I do not have a computer science background, and I do not know how to code. I also struggled with mathematics and physics before. Many parts of this project were explored with the help of LingTai AI, a system developed by my friend Zesen Huang. I asked it to help me investigate, research, organize, and process the ideas.

I may be someone who likes to think in unusual directions. While using LingTai, I gradually felt that an agent should not only be “a tool that answers questions.” It is more like a working system that can extend outward, recover its work, and consolidate what it learns. Knowledge should not only branch outward endlessly; it also needs to return to a center. A task should not end with just one file; the experience, templates, mistakes, and methods should be gathered back into the system.

That is where this sentence came from:

> **The net gives reach; the sphere gives body. Mind and spirit return as one; I become whole as a circle.**

I am not a computer science professional, and this idea is still exploratory. If anything here is immature or not rigorous enough, I sincerely welcome criticism and suggestions from people in computer science, AI, engineering, agent systems, and related fields. Thank you very much.

**Runyuan Wang**  
M.S. in Nutrition and Food Hygiene, Kunming Medical University (graduated)  
Chinese Registered Dietitian  
Member of Yunnan Astronomy Enthusiasts Association  
Currently working hard to learn AI
